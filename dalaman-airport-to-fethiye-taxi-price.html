Taxi or private transfer from Dalaman Airport to Fethiye?

Both options can work depending on your travel style. For many visitors, private transfer is easier because the details are arranged before arrival.

Airport taxi

Suitable for travellers who prefer to arrange transportation after landing and leave immediately without pre-booking.

Private transfer

Useful for families, late-night arrivals, travellers with luggage and guests who want a confirmed fixed price before travel.

Shared shuttle

May be cheaper for solo travellers, but usually includes waiting time, other passengers and multiple hotel stops.
