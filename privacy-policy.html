Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GoToFethiye collects, uses, stores and protects personal data when you use our website or request a private airport transfer.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

GoToFethiye respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and share personal information when you visit our website, contact us, request a quotation, make a reservation or use our private airport transfer services.

GoToFethiye primarily serves customers located in the European Union, the United Kingdom and other international destinations travelling to Turkey.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you contact us, request a price, make a reservation or communicate with us regarding transfer services.

Contact Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • WhatsApp number

Travel Information

  • Arrival airport
  • Flight number
  • Arrival date and time
  • Pickup and drop-off location
  • Destination address
  • Number of passengers
  • Luggage information
  • Child seat requests, if applicable

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage data

Payment Information

If online payments become available, payment information may be processed by trusted third-party payment providers such as Stripe, Mollie, PayPal or similar services.

GoToFethiye does not store complete credit card details on its own servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide price quotations
  • Arrange airport transfer services
  • Confirm reservations
  • Communicate with customers before and after travel
  • Coordinate with local transportation partners in Turkey
  • Monitor flight arrivals where applicable
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve website performance and customer experience
  • Comply with legal and administrative obligations

5. Legal Basis For Processing

Where applicable under GDPR, we process personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract or pre-contractual request
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Consent, where required

6. Sharing Information With Transfer Partners

To provide airport transfer services, we may share necessary booking information with local transportation providers operating in Turkey.

Information shared may include:

  • Passenger name
  • Arrival details
  • Flight information
  • Destination address
  • Passenger count
  • Contact telephone number

Only information necessary to perform the booked service is shared.

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

7. International Data Transfers

Because GoToFethiye is operated from Belgium and works with transportation partners located in Turkey, certain personal information may be transferred internationally when necessary to complete a booking.

Such transfers are limited to information required for service delivery.

8. GDPR Rights

If you are located within the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

You can submit privacy-related requests by contacting us using the details provided on this page.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Deliver requested services
  • Maintain business records
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Prevent fraud or misuse

When data is no longer required, it will be deleted or anonymized where reasonably possible.

10. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, monitor website performance and understand how visitors use the site.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or loss.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

  • Social media platforms

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

The latest version will always be available on this page.
