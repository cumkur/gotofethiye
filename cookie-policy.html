3. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website.

They can help a website remember basic information, improve performance, understand visitor behaviour and provide a better browsing experience.

4. Types Of Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly.

These cookies may be used for basic website functions such as page navigation, security, loading pages correctly and remembering basic preferences.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

They may show which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, which routes are popular and how users interact with the website.

If analytics tools such as Google Analytics are added to the website, they may use cookies to collect anonymous or aggregated usage information.

Communication Cookies

Our website may include links or buttons that allow visitors to contact us through WhatsApp, phone, email or other communication channels.

Third-party services such as WhatsApp may use their own cookies or tracking technologies according to their own policies.

Payment Cookies

If online payment options become available, trusted payment providers may use cookies or similar technologies to process payments, prevent fraud and secure transactions.

These payment providers may include Stripe, Mollie, PayPal or similar services.

Marketing Cookies

If marketing tools, advertising platforms or remarketing services are added in the future, they may use cookies to measure campaign performance or show relevant advertisements.

Non-essential marketing cookies should only be used where legally permitted and, where required, with user consent.

5. Why We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the website functioning correctly
  • Improve website speed and performance
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Improve route pages and booking experience
  • Measure SEO and marketing performance
  • Support secure online payments, if payment features are added
  • Improve customer communication

Our website may use or link to third-party services. These services may use their own cookies or similar technologies.

7. Cookie Consent

Essential cookies may be used because they are necessary for the website to function.

For non-essential cookies, such as analytics or marketing cookies, user consent may be required depending on applicable privacy and cookie laws.

Important: If Google Analytics, advertising pixels or other marketing tools are added to the website, a cookie consent banner may be required before those non-essential cookies are activated.

8. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Receive a warning before cookies are stored
  • Allow cookies only for selected websites

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the website.

Some cookies or similar technologies may collect information that can be considered personal data, such as IP address, device information or browsing behaviour.
